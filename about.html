About Me

Designer & Developer

I'm a designer who loves finding the balance between creativity and technology. I'm drawn to projects that explore how design can make everyday moments more meaningful whether through visuals, interaction, or storytelling. My background in design and computer science helps me connect creativity with clarity, and I enjoy experimenting with different tools and ideas to see what they can become.

Design, for me, is about curiosity, trying, failing, learning, and finding small details that make something feel right. When I'm not designing, I'm usually watching movies, catching up on TV shows, or spending time with my cats, who keep me company (and occasionally sit on my keyboard).
