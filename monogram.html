Monogram Design

Adobe Illustrator

September 2021 - February 2022

This project explores creating an original monogram by combining two letters or symbols into a single cohesive form. The goal was to experiment with how letterforms interact, overlap, and transform to produce a unique graphic mark. Monograms are often used in logos and identity systems, and this work focuses on developing a design that is both visually balanced and conceptually unified.
