I'm a designer who's genuinely curious about people, how they think, what they notice, and what makes an experience feel simple or complicated. I pay attention to small details and enjoy understanding why something feels clear, comfortable, or a bit off. My interest in different cultures, travel, and the way people share their stories influences a lot of how I see design.

Outside of work, I'm at my calmest when I'm at home with my cats, and that quiet time helps me reset and look at things with a clearer perspective. I like creating designs that reflect that same feeling, thoughtful, warm, and intentional, without unnecessary noise.
