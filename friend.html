E-waste Sculpture

Friend

Timeline
February 2025
Role
Designer & Maker
Tools
Soldering iron, hot glue, salvaged electronics
Team
Duo project — made with a friend

Friend is a small alien we built entirely out of things that had already stopped working — a dead milk frother, a car key fob, a handful of old resistors, and every loose wire we could find on the table.

We started with a box of parts that no longer worked and just started asking what each piece could still become. A milk frother turned into a torso. Old resistors, taped end to end, became arms. A car key fob became a head, its soldered wires left standing up like hair, or maybe antennae, we never quite agreed which.

We weren't trying to make it do anything. We just wanted to see what happens when you treat spare parts like they deserve a second life instead of a drawer. By the time we'd glued its name across a ring of old keyboard keys, it had stopped feeling like junk and started feeling like it was looking back at us. So we called it Friend.
