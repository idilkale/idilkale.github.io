Project Overview

1. Project Overview

Armoni is a full-scale branding project that explores how visual identity can shape emotional connection between a brand and its audience. The project includes a complete brand system: logo design, mascot development, product applications, website design, and environmental branding for both indoor and outdoor spaces. The goal was to create a unified and memorable visual identity that reflects harmony, balance, and accessibility.

This project was completed as a comprehensive design study and developed using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Figma.

Brand Strategy

2. Concept & Brand Philosophy

The name Armoni comes from the idea of harmony and balance—both visually and conceptually. The brand aims to create a cohesive experience across different touchpoints, where color, form, and typography work together without visual noise.

The design direction focuses on:

  • Smooth curves inspired by natural movement
  • A modern but friendly tone
  • A warm, inviting color palette
  • Consistent spacing and rhythm across layouts

The brand voice emphasizes clarity, friendliness, and approachability, preventing the identity from feeling overly corporate or sterile.

Logo Design

3. Logo Design

The logo was designed using simple geometric forms to communicate balance and fluid movement. Rounded shapes and soft edges create a sense of comfort and approachability, while the structured composition reflects order and stability. Multiple logo variations were developed for different applications, including horizontal, stacked, and icon-only formats.

The spacing system and construction grid ensure scalability and consistency across digital and print formats.

Mascot Design

4. Mascot Design

A mascot was created to bring personality to the brand and strengthen emotional engagement. The character reflects the name Armoni through soft shapes, gentle facial expressions, and a rounded silhouette. The mascot appears across product packaging, environmental graphics, marketing materials, and promotional content, helping the brand feel playful and relatable.

Different poses and expressions were designed to communicate specific moods, allowing the mascot to support multiple use cases without losing visual cohesion.

Brand System

5. Color & Typography System

The color palette combines warm, harmonious tones with neutral supporting shades. These colors create visual rhythm across touchpoints and maintain brand consistency while allowing flexibility in layout design.

Typography follows a functional and clean style. Fira Sans is used throughout the brand identity for both body text and headings, while Montserrat serves as an alternative typeface, particularly in logo applications. This consistent typographic system ensures visual cohesion while maintaining readability across different scales and applications. Hierarchy guidelines were defined for print, web, and signage to ensure clarity across scales.

Print Collateral

6. Stationery & Corporate Collateral

As part of the branding system, a complete stationery package was designed to represent the brand in professional and formal contexts. The package includes business cards, envelopes, presentation folders, letterhead layouts, and printed document materials. Each item follows the core visual guidelines of the brand, using consistent spacing, color accents, and mascot placement.

Product Design

7. Product Design

Armoni's visual identity extends to custom-designed physical products that reflect the brand's core values and aesthetic language. These items focus on daily usability and tactile interaction, allowing users to experience the brand beyond digital environments. Product designs include functional items that integrate the mascot, color palette, and illustration style in a refined and user-centered way.

Each product follows consistent visual guidelines such as spacing, rounded forms, and soft color transitions. The designs emphasize harmony, comfort, and familiarity, reinforcing Armoni's brand character through form, material, and surface graphics.

These products demonstrate how the brand can exist as a tangible experience, not just a visual identity.

Merchandise

8. Promotional Items & Branded Merchandise

In addition to product design, a series of promotional items were created to support brand awareness and communication campaigns. These items serve as accessible touchpoints that help the brand reach wider audiences in events, pop-ups, and social settings.

The promotional collection includes items such as apparel, tote bags, stickers, notebooks, accessories, and mascot-themed giveaways. Unlike the functional product line, promotional pieces focus on visibility, emotional engagement, and playful expression of the mascot.

The goal of this collection is to strengthen brand recognition while enabling users to interact with Armoni in a more casual and social way.

Environmental Design

9. Environmental Branding

The brand was applied to both indoor and outdoor spaces, including storefront signage, display walls, interior graphics, and wayfinding elements. The goal was to create an immersive brand atmosphere that users would experience physically, not just visually.

Outdoor applications emphasize visibility and recognizability from a distance, while indoor environments focus on comfort, color harmony, and storytelling.

Digital Experience

10. Website Design

A responsive website was designed to reflect the brand's visual language across digital interactions. The site includes:

  • Home page with brand introduction
  • Product pages
  • Mascot illustrations
  • Visual storytelling sections
  • Clean navigation and UI structure

The UI is built around large visuals, generous spacing, and soft transitions to support the overall brand tone. The website was prototyped using Figma, with a focus on accessibility, legibility, and mobile-first layout principles.

Print Design

11. Brochure Design

A promotional brochure was created to present Armoni's brand identity and visual system in a structured print format. The layout prioritizes clarity, clean hierarchy, and balanced compositions, using modular grids and consistent typographic styling to reinforce the concept of visual harmony. Since the brochure focuses on communicating the brand's formal and professional tone.

Reflection

What this project taught me

Building Armoni taught me that consistency across touchpoints matters more than any single hero image. A mascot only works when spacing, color, and tone stay disciplined everywhere it appears.

If I continued, I would test the identity in more real-world contexts like signage mockups and motion guidelines.
