Catastrophe

Catastrophe visualizes a dystopian world transformed by the devastating effects of global warming. Created during a period when AI-generated imagery was still emerging, this project experiments with the virtual reality potential of DALL-E to explore how technology can visualize future environmental crises. Through this speculative lens, the work aims to provoke reflection on humanity's role in shaping the planet's fate.
